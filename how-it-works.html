How It Works

Learn how to buy and sell on BidLuxe, the premier online auction platform.

Buying on BidLuxe

1. Create an Account

Sign up for a free BidLuxe account to start browsing auctions and save your favorite items.

2. Browse Auctions

Explore our extensive collection of auctions across various categories. Use filters to find exactly what you're looking for.

Browse Auctions

3. Pay Entry Fee

To participate in an auction, you'll need to pay a 10% entry fee based on the item's base price. This ensures only serious bidders participate and creates a better experience for everyone.

The entry fee is non-refundable but demonstrates your commitment to the bidding process.

3
4

4. Place Bids

Once you've paid the entry fee, you can place bids on the auction. Each auction has a minimum bid increment that you must meet or exceed.

You'll receive notifications when you're outbid so you can stay competitive.

5. Win & Pay

If you're the highest bidder when the auction ends, congratulations! You'll receive a notification and instructions to complete your purchase.

We offer secure payment options and will coordinate with the seller for shipping or pickup arrangements.
